FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I enroll my child at Enefem Schools?

To enroll your child at Enefem Schools, please visit our Admissions page on our website. You will find detailed information on the enrollment process, required documents, and contact information for our admissions office. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or need assistance.

What are the school's operating hours?

Our regular school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday. However, please check our Academic Calendar for any special schedule changes, early dismissals, or school holidays.

Is there a dress code at Enefem Schools?

Yes, we have a dress code to maintain a positive and respectful learning environment. You can find detailed information about our dress code policy in the Student Handbook, available on our website.

What is the school's policy on bullying and student safety?

Enefem Schools is committed to providing a safe and inclusive environment for all students. We have a comprehensive anti-bullying policy and reporting procedures. You can find more information in our Student Handbook or contact our guidance counselor for support and guidance.

What extracurricular activities are available for students?

Enefem Schools offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, including clubs, sports teams, arts programs, and more. Visit our Extracurricular Activities page to explore the options available and learn how to get involved
