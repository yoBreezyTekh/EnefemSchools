Achievements

At the extraordinary meeting of the Board of Governors of Enefem International Schools held at No. 19, Amobi Nwanah street, Megida-Ayobo, Lagos on April 2nd 2012. The decision to set up Enefem Nursery, Primary & Seondary schools was made and approved. The official opening (inauguration) ceremony of the school took place on September 12th 2012. The institution, effectively began as a schools system on the 17th of the same month with the three schools -Nursery, Primary, Secondary. The total population of the students that started was about 40. The pioneer staff were Mrs Offison Gloria, Mrs Adeniji Victoria, Mr Adeleye Adelowo, Miss Ponmile Faith, Mr Akinboro Akinola and Mrs Akinlade Eniola.


OUR OBJECTIVES

ENEFEM International School is a translation of a message received from God to reality. We are specifically instructed to join the league of people bringing up children under the following terms of reference:

(a) To provide good and qualitative education towards the attainment of academic success.

(b) To raise a generation of tomorrow's leaders that have the fear of God in them.

(c) To train and develop the spirit of job creators rather than job seekers in every child through the use of modern technological materials, thereby making them to be self-reliant.

OUR VISION

(a) To deliver standard and qualitative education to every students that passes through ENEFEM International Schools in partnership with other stakeholders and make education the pivot of national development.

(b) To contibute our quota in providing jobs opportunities for the masses, thereby reducing the crime rate in the country.

OUR MISSION

To operate a transparent and efficient teaching methodology that optimizes academic success and national development.
